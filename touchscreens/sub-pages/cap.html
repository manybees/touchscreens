Capacitive touch sensors measure electrostatic fields and requires a touch panel that includes a capacitor. Additional sensing pads and software algorithms are also used to distinguish between intended and false touches. Hovering can perform touch commands which is not possible with resistive sensors. Smartphones and tablets use this technology the most.

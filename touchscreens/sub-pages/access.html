Touchscreens and the Design Industry

Drawing programs like ProCreate use tablets that depend on capacitive touch. The app is user friendly for all skillsets from beginner to professional. Without capacitive touch, a successful painting program like ProCreate wouldnt exist.

Tablets have changed the design industry as they provide a workable digital surface. With the advancement of capacitive and resistive technology, drawing tablets have only gotten better and have become a staple tool for digital artists, animators and designers alike.
